It happened in an instant. Millions of people all over the world have fallen unconscious. Scientists are
baffled. No one can seem to find a correlation between everyone who vanished.


But things are even more mysterious for our good friend Jack.


When he goes online to check on his community, he finds thousands of messages containing nothing
but two words: SAVE US. He receives a strange email, which immediately knocks him out upon
clicking on it.


Once awake, he discovers that he's somehow ended up inside his own computer. Now Jack must
battle through his own games to save his subscribers from someone calling themself the BOSS...
